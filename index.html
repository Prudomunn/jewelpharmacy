Welcome To Jewel Pharmarcy

We know how important it is to feel confident in your pharmacy. Your care is important to us, which is why we take our time to help you understand your prescription and answer any questions you have. Our number one priority is making sure you are getting the correct prescription and that you understand how and when to take it. Our pharmacist personally attends to each prescription, checking for accuracy, and any potential interactions. We know you have many choices when it comes to where you get your medication, and we are proud to be the premier pharmacy for Harare City and the surrounding area. Stop by or call us if you have questions about switching to us or about your medications. We also have a wide selection of nonprescription, over the counter medications and remedies to help you feel your best.
